---
title: Adding a New Documentation Page
description: Create and organize docs in Snappytb.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Click your project name from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a page">
    Click "Add Doc" or the "+" icon in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in details">
    Name your doc and add content in the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Press "Save" to update your documentation instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
