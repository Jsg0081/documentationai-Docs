## Overview

Snappytb is the world's best trial balance software, powered by AI to help you import trial balance data, automatically map accounts to IRS Form categories, and export reports anywhere. You close books faster without spreadsheets or manual cleanup.

Designed for accounting professionals and firms, Snappytb handles everything from data normalization to secure multi-entity consolidation. Process thousands of rows with real-time feedback, get smart tax mapping suggestions based on your firm's history, and export to CSV, Excel, or PDF in one click.

Whether you work at a small firm or manage enterprise-scale consolidations, Snappytb delivers speed, security, and simplicity.

## Who Uses Snappytb

You are an accountant, CPA, or firm controller looking to eliminate manual trial balance work. Snappytb targets:

- Small to mid-sized accounting firms handling 10-100 clients
- Enterprise teams managing multi-entity closes
- Solo practitioners needing quick IRS mapping for tax prep

With responsive design, it works on desktops, tablets, and mobiles for office or field use.
