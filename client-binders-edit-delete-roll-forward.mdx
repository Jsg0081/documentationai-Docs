## Overview

Binders are the client's year folders shown on the client detail page. Each binder represents a fiscal year (or period) and opens into its own engagement workspace.

Use this page to quickly find, open, edit, delete, and roll forward binders.

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Binders live on the client detail page. Each binder is scoped to a single client and fiscal year or accounting period.
</Callout>

## Find and open binders

### Where to find binders

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the client" icon="folder" title-type="p">
    Open a client from the sidebar or dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Locate the Binders section" icon="monitor" title-type="p">
    Scroll to the Binders section in the client detail page. You can toggle between tile view and table view using the view switcher.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Open a binder

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the binder" icon="play-circle" title-type="p">
    - In tile view, click any binder card

    - In table view, click any binder row
  </Step>

  <Step title="Work in the engagement workspace" icon="code" title-type="p">
    You will be taken to that binder's engagement workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Edit a binder

You can edit a binder's year, name, and accounting period.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the binder" icon="folder" title-type="p">
    Find the binder you want to change in the Binders list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the edit dialog" icon="edit" title-type="p">
    Click the Edit icon (pencil).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update fields" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    Update the fields you need to change.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save changes" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    Click **Save**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Expandable title="Edit dialog fields" default-open="true">
  Fields in the Edit dialog:

  - **Year**: The fiscal year of the binder. Must be a number (for example, `2025`).

  - **Name**: Display name for the binder (optional).

  - **Accounting Period From**: Start date for the period (optional).

  - **Accounting Period To**: End date for the period (optional).
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Editing tips" default-open="false">
  - If the **Year** field is not a valid number, the system will reject the change.

  - Accounting period dates are optional but recommended for clarity.
</Expandable>

## Delete a binder

Deleting a binder is permanent and cannot be undone.

<Callout kind="danger" collapsed="false">
  **Permanent deletion**

  - Deleting a binder is permanent and cannot be undone

  - Make sure you picked the correct binder and year before confirming
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the binder" icon="folder" title-type="p">
    Find the binder you want to delete in the Binders list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click delete" icon="trash" title-type="p">
    Click the Delete icon (trash).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm permanent deletion" icon="alert-triangle" title-type="p">
    Carefully review the binder details, then confirm the deletion in the confirmation dialog.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  If you are unsure whether you will need the binder later, edit it instead of deleting to preserve history.
</Callout>

## Roll forward a binder

Roll Forward creates a new binder for the next year using the current binder as a template.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose the source binder" icon="folder" title-type="p">
    Find the binder you want to roll forward in the Binders list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open roll forward" icon="play-circle" title-type="p">
    Click the Roll Forward icon (fast-forward).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the new binder" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    Fill out the roll forward fields for the new binder.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create the new binder" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    Click **Roll Forward**. After a successful roll forward, SnappyTB will take you directly to the new binder.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Expandable title="Roll forward dialog fields" default-open="true">
  Fields in the Roll Forward dialog:

  - **Target Year**: The fiscal year for the new binder (required).

  - **Binder Name**: Optional display name for the new binder.

  - **Accounting Period From**: Start date for the new period.

  - **Accounting Period To**: End date for the new period.

  - **Copy Account Mappings**: Toggle to carry forward tax mappings.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What roll forward copies" default-open="false">
  What Roll Forward copies:

  - Chart of accounts

  - Perm File documents and folders

  - Tax mappings if **Copy Account Mappings** is enabled
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What roll forward does not copy" default-open="false">
  What Roll Forward does not copy:

  - Trial balance values (start at zero)

  - Journal entries

  - Workpapers other than Perm File
</Expandable>
